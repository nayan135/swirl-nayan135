My Journey

Namaste! I'm Nayan Acharya from Kathmandu, Nepal. My fascination with coding began unexpectedly in 2021 when a friend challenged me to build a simple website. What started as curiosity quickly became a genuine passion after I saw my first HTML page come to life.

The year 2022 marked a turning point when I chose to pursue Computer Engineering despite my parents initially hoping I'd follow a more traditional path. That same year, I stumbled upon GitHub after a frustrating experience losing all my code to a corrupted drive – a lesson I'll never forget!

By mid-2023, I'd grown ambitious enough to tackle full-stack projects, learning through countless late nights and debugging sessions. All the hard work culminated in achieving a 3.72 GPA in my SEE exams, which opened doors I never thought possible.

Today, having completed Grade 11, I'm balancing my studies with a leadership role at YPSTEM while working on my side project – a platform connecting rural Nepali students with tech education resources. I believe technology should serve everyone, not just those in cities with easy access.
